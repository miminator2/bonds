Types of Bonds

Substances have different types of bonds connecting the elements. Each bond varies on the properties of the element.

Covalent Bonds

Covalent bonds are bonds that are formed between elements that share valence electrons.

Ionic Bonds

Ionic bonds are bonds that are formed between elements that do not share valence electrons. They completely take and give, which results in charged ions.
